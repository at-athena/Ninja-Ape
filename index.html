Tokenomics

Token Name: Ninja Ape (NJA)

Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 NJA

  • Initial Liquidity Pool

    40% of the total supply allocated to provide liquidity on decentralized exchanges (DEXs) for initial trading.

  • Community Rewards

    25% of the total supply reserved for rewards, incentives, and giveaways to foster community engagement and growth.

  • Development and Marketing

    20% of the total supply allocated for project development, marketing campaigns, partnerships, and exchange listings.

  • Reserve Fund

    10% of the total supply held in a reserve fund to support future development, security, and unforeseen circumstances.

  • Team and Advisors

    5% of the total supply allocated for the project's core team and advisors, with a vesting period to align their interests with the long-term success of Ninja Ape.

Token Utility

- NJA can be used for transactions within the Ninja Ape ecosystem.
- Staking NJA may allow holders to earn additional tokens as rewards.

Token Burning

A portion of transaction fees or a regular token burn mechanism may be implemented to reduce the total supply of NJA over time, potentially increasing its value.

Community Governance

NJA holders can actively participate in decision-making processes through on-chain governance, influencing the direction of the Ninja Ape project.

Economic Model

Ninja Ape's tokenomics aim to create an engaging and rewarding environment for its community, with incentives for long-term holding, active participation, and community-driven development.
